Contract Staffing

Junction Tech is empaneled with some of the best Staffing Partners in the market to support its sourcing process for the best candidates. Contract Staffing is dependent completely on the Staffing Partners . Therefore we have a stringent evaluating process for empaneled partners.

After a thorough evaluation, the Contract Staffing Partners will recommend qualified personnel through the 'Synergy' web-based recruitment system to Junction Tech from time to time and agree to do so as per the terms and conditions for a period of 12 months, after which they will be reviewed for renewal or termination.
