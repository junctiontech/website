Software Development

Junction Tech caters to start-ups, system integrators, software product & services companies and Fortune 500 enterprises when it comes to software development. Whether you want to launch a new project or modernize an existing application making the best use of the latest technology, we can handle your entire project from design through development into support.

We have been offering software development services to customers around the world for over 14 years. Junction Tech has built numerous applications for enterprises of varying sizes and has functioned as extended teams of these enterprises. We have accumulated in-depth expertise and experience around Web, Mobile, Cloud, CMS and E-commerce solutions..

Our software development approach is based on agile methodology, which is based on iterative and incremental development. It promotes adaptive planning, evolutionary development and delivery, a time-boxed iterative approach, and encourages rapid and flexible response to change.
